## Closing Perspective

The July 2026 landscape rewards **integration over addition**. Organizations treating PCI-DSS, NIST CSF 2.0, GDPR, DORA, and SEC rules as separate programs will face unsustainable cost structures and evidence gaps. The path forward is a **unified control framework** with:

1. **Shared control library** mapped to all applicable requirements
2. **Automated evidence collection** from source systems (cloud, code, identity, network)
3. **Continuous control monitoring** replacing point-in-time assessments
4. **Board-ready risk reporting** translating technical control status into business risk posture

The next quarter will differentiate organizations that have operationalized this integration from those still managing compliance in silos.
