## 6. Closing Perspective

**Q2 2026 marks the inflection point where "compliance" becomes "governance."** Regulators across the US, EU, and sectoral bodies have aligned on three non-negotiables:

1. **Accountability at the top** — Boards and CEOs personally liable for cyber and data risk oversight
2. **Evidence over attestation** — Continuous, automated control verification replacing annual point-in-time audits
3. **Ecosystem responsibility** — Organizations answer for the cyber posture of their entire supply chain

Organizations that treat these as **separate compliance projects** will face escalating costs, audit findings, and enforcement risk. Those that **architect a unified GRC operating model**—single control framework, automated evidence, quantified risk, and board-level governance—will convert regulatory pressure into **competitive resilience**.
