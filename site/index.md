## 1. Executive Summary

1. **Regulatory frameworks are converging globally**, with GDPR, CCPA, and emerging state-level U.S. privacy laws increasingly harmonizing around common data-handling principles — but diverging sharply on enforcement mechanisms and penalty structures.
