### 2.2 SOX — Expanding Into Algorithmic Financial Controls

The SEC has signaled through recent enforcement actions and staff guidance in May 2026 that SOX Section 404 internal control assessments must now explicitly address:

- **AI/ML models** used in financial forecasting, revenue recognition, and risk estimation
- **Automated reconciliation and reporting pipelines** where human review has been reduced or eliminated
- **Algorithmic audit trails** demonstrating model version control, input data integrity, and output validation

**Business Impact**: Public companies must update their Internal Control over Financial Reporting (ICFR) frameworks to include AI model governance. Audit committees should expect external auditors to request documentation of model risk management practices during upcoming audit cycles.

### 2.3 NIST Cybersecurity Framework — Governance Function Expansion

NIST released supplemental guidance in April–May 2026 expanding the **Govern (GV)** function introduced in CSF 2.0. The updates emphasize:

**Business Impact**: Organizations aligning to NIST CSF should conduct a gap analysis against the expanded GV function. Federal contractors and critical infrastructure entities face the most immediate compliance pressure.
