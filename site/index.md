### Cross-Sector Pattern: The "Compliance Multiplier Effect"
Organizations subject to **3+ major frameworks** (e.g., SOX + PCI-DSS + GDPR + ISO 27001) report **40–60% control overlap** but **disparate evidence collection processes**. This drives:
- Duplicate testing costs
- Inconsistent control language
- Audit fatigue
- Blind spots in non-overlapping areas (e.g., AI governance, OT security)
