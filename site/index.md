## 7. Closing Perspective

The July 2026 landscape rewards **integration over addition**. Organizations adding yet another framework-specific program will drown in cost and complexity. The leaders are those who:

1. **Map once, comply many** — A single control catalog serving DORA, NIS2, SEC, GDPR, PCI-DSS, ISO 27001, NIST CSF 2.0, and SOX simultaneously

5. **Align incentives** — Board metrics, CISO objectives, audit plans, and insurance underwriting all speaking the same risk language
