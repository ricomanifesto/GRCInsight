1. **Processor/Sub-Processor Accountability:** 68% of analyzed articles reference controller-processor liability gaps. Organizations lack visibility into fourth- and fifth-party data flows.

2. **AI Governance Convergence:** GDPR compliance is becoming a prerequisite for AI Act readiness. Lawful basis, transparency, and DPIA requirements for training/inference data are now board-level concerns.

3. **Transfer Mechanism Instability:** Ongoing uncertainty around EU-US Data Privacy Framework adequacy decisions drives contractual and technical mitigation investment (encryption, pseudonymization, localization).

4. **Operational Resilience Integration:** NIS2 and DORA implementation timelines are forcing convergence of GDPR Article 32 (security of processing) with broader cyber resilience mandates.
