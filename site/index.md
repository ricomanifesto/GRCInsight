**Financial Services** remains the most exposed sector in May 2026. The convergence of SOX ITGC scrutiny, GDPR enforcement on algorithmic trading/AI credit scoring, and full PCI-DSS v4.0.1 enforcement creates a multi-vector compliance challenge that requires dedicated cross-functional governance.
